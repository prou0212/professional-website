My Journey

The following year, I landed a part-time position at Jewel 98.5 working promotions and as a casual on-air personality at their sister station Jewel 107.7 in Hawkesbury, Ontario. I was moving up in the world, but everything changed not just for me, but for everybody!

Change of Career

In March 2020, COVID-19 swept through Canada and the entire world. With lockdowns in effect, I was let go and returned to work as a server in a retirement home. Having always excelled at math in high school, I was encouraged to apply for Computer Programming at Algonquin College in early 2024. After 7 years since my last graduation, I got ACCEPTED into the program! Starting from nothing, I went through the most challenging period of my life but achieved incredible grades through determination, passion, and help from amazing friends. There's only one way to go from here up!
