All About ME!

From Radio Broadcasting to Computer Programming, Here is my journey to where I am here today. This is my story.

The Year of 1996

Born in Kanata, Ontario, I've called the Ottawa region home my entire life. In 2001, my family made the move to Winchester, and in 2016 I found my way back to Ottawa — the city that's always felt like the centre of my story.

Early Life in Ottawa

I studied Radio Broadcasting at Algonquin College from 2015 to 2017, which opened the door to a career I loved. I went on to work at Jewel 98.5 in promotions and as an on-air personality at Jewel 107.7 — connecting with people, building a presence, and telling stories through sound. Like so many others, my time in radio came to an unexpected end when COVID-19 hit in 2020. It was a chapter closing, but it turned out to be the beginning of something new.
